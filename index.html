About Me

Bridging Academia and Innovation

Currently serving as Assistant Professor at Akal University, Punjab, I bring cutting-edge AI research into the classroom, inspiring the next generation of computer scientists and innovators.

With a Master's degree in Computer Science from Central University of Punjab and a passion for Machine Learning applications in real-world problems, my work focuses on making AI accessible and impactful.

My research in agricultural disease detection using deep learning has garnered over 190 citations, reflecting its significance in bridging technology with sustainable farming practices.
